## Vision
Tap & Tell is a private, minimal, and elegant digital guestbook for homes.
Guests tap their phone on an NFC-enabled object and instantly leave
a photo and a personal message.

## Target Audience
- Families
- Homeowners
- Private event hosts

## Core User Flow
1. Guest taps NFC tag
2. App opens
3. Guest leaves name, photo, and message
4. Confirmation screen
